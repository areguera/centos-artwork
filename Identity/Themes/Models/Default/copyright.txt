Default design models for The CentOS Project. Design models provide the
structural information (e.g., image dimensions, translation markers, etc.) the
centos-art.sh script needs to produce themes.

Directories

    Distro/     Design models for different releases of CentOS distribution
                and their components (e.g., Anaconda, Firstboot, Gdm, Grub,
                Gsplash, Kdm, Ksplash, Rhgb and Syslinux, etc.).

    Concept/    Design models for artistic motif concept presentations.

    Promo/      Design models for promotion stuff (e.g., installation media,
                posters, etc.).
